Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Scripnote, subscriptions, and account support. Explore our FAQ to get help fast!

How does the free trial work?

The free trial lasts 14 days and gives you full access to all features. When the trial ends, you can decide if you want to continue using it by starting the subscription associated with your account type.

Are there any additional charges?

No, the monthly or annual subscription fee covers all features. There are no hidden fees or additional charges.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes, we do. For both individual and company account types, annual subscriptions include 2 months free per year. You pay for 10 months and get a full year.

What if I cancel my subscription?

If you cancel, you’ll still have access to Scripnote until the end of your billing cycle. After that, your account features will be deactivated, but your data will be securely stored for future reactivation.

Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied?

Scripnote provides a flexible cancellation policy, allowing you to request a refund within 14 days of your billing date.

Can I have both account types?

Yes, you can, but you will need to create two separate accounts. One account type cannot switch between individual and company plans.

Do you offer team plans for companies?

Not yet, but we’re working on adding team subscription options for companies that require multiple user accounts. Stay tuned for updates on this feature.

What payment methods do you accept?

Scripnote processes payments securely through Stripe. You can pay using major credit and debit cards, and in many regions, Stripe also supports Apple Pay, Google Pay, and local payment methods.

Frequently asked questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Scripnote, subscriptions, and account support. Explore our FAQ to get help fast!

What is Scripnote exactly?

Scripnote is a financial clarity platform that helps you track spending, monitor cashflow, and understand your financial habits in one place.

Who is Scripnote meant for?

Scripnote is built for individuals and businesses who want simple, organized tools to manage expenses, savings, and long-term financial planning.

How much does Scripnote cost?

We offer flexible monthly and yearly plans for both individual and company accounts. Visit the pricing page for full details.

Why is the company subscription price higher?

Company plans include advanced collaboration, reporting, and oversight tools designed for teams and larger financial workflows.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes. You can start a free trial anytime to explore the full experience.

Can I connect my bank to Scripnote?

Secure bank connections are supported, allowing you to sync transactions and see an accurate view of your financial activity.

Is my information safe?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption and security practices to keep your data protected.

Etymology

As you have probably guessed, the name Scripnote is a blend of two words: scrip and note.

Together, they reflect our mission to combine financial insight with meaningful record-keeping, empowering you to manage, grow, and fully understand the value behind each transaction.

Scrip historically refers to a certificate or temporary document representing financial value, often used as a form of currency in earlier economies.

Note, on the other hand, signifies a record or written reminder. An essential way to capture details, insights, and ideas that guide decision-making.

Our beginnings

Scripnote was created to address a pressing global issue: the widespread lack of financial literacy and the costly financial mistakes that result from it. Too many people struggle with managing their money effectively, often due to limited access to financial education and tools that truly support decision-making.

Our mission is to provide a platform that empowers users with accessible knowledge, personalized insights, and the confidence to take control of their financial future.

Cristian Radu Chief executive officer
